Business Problem

A bike retail company wanted to gain clear visibility into its sales performance, discount impact, category contribution, and store efficiency to support data-driven decision-making.

  • Which products and categories generate the highest revenue?
  • How do discounts affect net revenue and profitability?
  • Which stores and staff contribute most to sales?
  • Are there seasonal and weekday sales patterns?

Dataset & Data Model

The analysis is based on a real-world BikeStore retail dataset consisting of 9 relational tables designed using a star-schema approach.

  • Fact Table: order_items (transaction-level sales)
  • Dimensions: customers, products, categories, brands, stores, staff, orders
  • Grain: Order-item level

Power BI Dashboard

SQL-validated metrics are transformed into an interactive Power BI dashboard.

  • KPI cards for revenue, orders, customers, discount %, net revenue
  • Monthly & weekly revenue trends
  • Category, product, store & staff performance

Key Business Insights

  • Mountain Bikes generate the highest revenue but carry margin risk.
  • Revenue is concentrated across a limited set of products.
  • Baldwin Bikes significantly outperforms other stores.
  • Sales show strong seasonality.
  • Weekends and Mondays outperform mid-week.

Business Recommendations

  • Adopt category-specific discount strategies.
  • Scale high-profit categories.
  • Replicate best practices from top stores.
  • Run targeted seasonal campaigns.
