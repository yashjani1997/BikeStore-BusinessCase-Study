BikeStore Sales Performance Analysis

Executive Data Analytics Case Study using SQL & Power BI

Consulting-Style • Business Ready

Business Problem

A bike retail company required a consolidated and reliable view of its sales performance to support strategic decision-making.

  • Which products and categories drive the highest revenue?
  • How do discounts impact net revenue and margins?
  • Which stores and staff contribute most to sales?
  • Are there identifiable seasonal and weekday patterns?

Dataset & Data Model

  • Fact Table: order_items
  • Dimensions: customers, products, categories, brands, stores, staff, orders
  • Grain: Order-item level

Key Business Insights

Mountain Bikes generate the highest revenue but increase margin risk.
Revenue is concentrated across a limited number of products.
Baldwin Bikes significantly outperforms other stores.
Sales show strong seasonality and weekday patterns.

Business Recommendations

Adopt category-specific discount strategies.
Scale high-performing and high-margin categories.
Replicate best practices from top-performing stores.
Plan targeted seasonal and weekday campaigns.
